Personal website
======
A postdoctoral researcher at the [School of Earth and Space Science and Technology](https://esst.whu.edu.cn/), [Wuhan University](https://www.whu.edu.cn/). During my PhD, my research focused on low-latitude ionosphere–thermosphere coupling. My current postdoctoral research focuses on magnetosphere–ionosphere coupling.
